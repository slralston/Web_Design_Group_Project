Les Girafes

A herd of monumental and curious red giraffes will move nonchalantly through the streets of Galway led by an operatic Diva! The giraffes adore her!

Compagnie Off is one of the most illustrious street theatre companies in Europe combining circus, spectacle, opera, live performance and monumental staging.

These spectacular animals, who will enthral audiences of all ages, are joined by a troupe of circus ring masters adding to the excitement and energy of this magical street parade.

Spectacle and chaos await in equal measure as this show transports you to the fantastic world of Compagnie Off.

The Parade will travel from Eyre Square to Spanish Arch.
