2023 Music Shows

Fat Freddy’s Drop

Fat Freddy’s Drop, a group from Wellington, New Zealand are infamous throughout the world for their distinctive fusion of soul, jazz, reggae, and electronic music, and we are delighted to announce they will be performing in Galway this summer. Having been established in 1999, Fat Freddy's Drop has gained a devoted audience around the world thanks to their distinctive and exciting performances, and we are excited for them to perform at the Big Top.

Kaiser Chiefs

The Kaiser Chiefs are a British indie rock band that have been extremely popular in Ireland since the turn of the millennium. From 'Ruby' to 'I Predict a Riot', fans of the band can expect to hear other household known classics when the band come to Galway in July. The group is renowned and popular, and we are anticipating their exciting and captivating live performance.

Bell X1

Galway International Arts Festival are extremely excited to announce the upcoming performance of Bell X1. The Irish band are fan favourites throughout the country with numerous hits throughout the last two decades. From songs like ‘Flame’, ‘Rocky Took a Lover’ and ‘The Great Defector’, we hope that you can join us to hear the sound of nostalgia flow throughout the Big Top.

Kettama

Galway born DJ and producer Kettama made a name for himself in 2018 when he took the electronic music scene in the country to new levels. Because of his distinctive sound, which combines elements of house and techno, he has earned popularity in the electronic music scene. Kettama is also known for his vibrant and vivacious live performances, which frequently incorporate both his own works and songs by other artists. We anticipate that when "Raw Cuts" starts playing, the Big Top's roof will be blown off.
