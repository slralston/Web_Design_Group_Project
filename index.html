Experience the GIAF

Dance the night away

Capital of Ireland's west coast and site of one of the most thrilling summer festivals in the world, Galway City, welcomes you! We cordially invite you to join us for a memorable event rich with music, the arts, culture, and good times. As soon as the sun sets, the city comes to life with a renowned, thriving nightlife. You'll always find entertainment to keep you dancing into the early hours, from traditional Irish music to cutting-edge DJs, and big names performing at the Heineken Big Top.

Create lasting memories

Countless people throughout the world travel to Galway City each year to participate in the renowned Galway International Arts Festival. We can't wait to have you join us for the celebrations from July 17 to July 30 of this year. Galway has a distinctive allure that you won't find anywhere else thanks to its cobblestone streets, vibrant buildings, and busy pubs. Galway City is a location of endless curiosity and adventure, from the historic Spanish Arch to the magnificent Claddagh.

Make new friends

With a vibrant arts community and a strong student population, the city is a magnet for creativity and innovation. You'll get the chance to interact with students from all around the world, discover Irish culture, and create lifelong experiences. So why not come and hang out with us in Galway this summer? We promise you'll find something to adore in our lovely city, whether your interests are in music, art, history, or simply discovering a new area. Be ready for an exceptional summer adventure by making your travel plans right away.

Discover the city

Plan your stay for the Galway International Arts Festival by booking your tickets early and reserving accommodation. Explore the festival program in advance and plan which events you'd like to attend. Familiarize yourself with the city and festival venues, and arrange transportation as needed. Remember to take breaks and enjoy the city's attractions beyond the festival. Plan your stay with these tips to make the most of your visit to Galway.

Be part of the magic

Be part of the magic at the Galway International Arts Festival by experiencing the vibrant and diverse cultural scene of Galway. Immerse yourself in the festival's events and performances, connect with fellow attendees, and discover the unique atmosphere of the city. Whether you're a culture enthusiast or a first-time visitor, come and be part of the magic of the Galway International Arts Festival.
