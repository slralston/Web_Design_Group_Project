Nightlife

Drinks and Dancing

The Quays Bar

The Quays is a fantastic pub where you can have amazing Irish food and fresh oysters from Galway Bay every night of the week while you fussing to live traditional music. Enjoy!

The Front Door

The Front Door is the go-to place for Galway's nightlife, offering beverages till 2am and live music seven nights a week. Attend the celebration and mingle with the revellers in the city!

Blake's Bar

Blake's Bistro provides a superb meal at a reasonable price in a friendly and laid-back setting. Take a break from the bustle of the city and relax in a tranquil sanctuary.
