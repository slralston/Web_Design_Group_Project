Nightlife

Drinks and Dancing

The Quays Bar

The Quays hosts live traditional music sessions seven nights a week and serve quality traditional Irish cuisine, not to mention the delicious oysters straight from the Galway Bay. Most nights you will find locals and tourists enjoying the buzzing atmosphere. Therefore, if you are a solo traveler in Galway, stop by the Quays where there will always be a friendly face and a pint waiting!

The Front Door

The Front Door is the place where Galway's party people come to socialise in a city that's renowned for its nightlife. Each and every evening the bar is a hive of activity with drinks served until 2am. There is live music 7 nights a week with popular bands and DJs.

Blake's Bar

Blake's features all the elements of an authentic bistro, from a cozy, unpretentious atmosphere to delicious fare at a modest price and will transport guests to an oasis of calm from the hustle and bustle of busy city life.
