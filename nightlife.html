Nightlife

Drinks and Dancing

The Quays Bar

The Quays is a popular venue that offers quality traditional Irish cuisine and hosts live traditional music sessions seven nights a week. The atmosphere is vibrant, and tourists and locals alike frequent the place. So, if you are travelling alone, The Quays can be an excellent place to visit where you can find friendly faces and a pint to drink!

The Front Door

The Front Door is the perfect place for those who want to experience the lively nightlife of Galway. The bar is a hub of activity each night, serving drinks until 2 am. Popular bands and DJs perform live music every night, making it a must-visit place for partygoers.

Blake's Bar

Blake's bistro provides a cozy and relaxed atmosphere, where visitors can enjoy delicious and affordable food. The restaurant offers an escape from the city's hustle and bustle, providing guests with an oasis of calm.
