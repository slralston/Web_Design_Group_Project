Mars

UK artist Luke Jerram's latest work, "Mars," can be found at Nun's Island Distillery (H91 YA48) and is open daily from noon to 8 pm. The internally lit spherical sculpture is seven metres in diameter and displays 120dpi detailed NASA imagery of the Martian surface. At a scale of approximately 1:1 million, each centimetre of the sculpture represents 10 kilometres of the planet's surface, allowing viewers to explore Mars from a bird's-eye view. The artwork features every valley, crater, volcano, and mountain in perfect detail, providing a glimpse into this incredible planet.

Les Girafes

The Compagnie Off street theatre company, known for combining circus, spectacle, opera, live performance, and monumental staging, will lead a street parade featuring a herd of massive and curious red giraffes, accompanied by an operatic Diva. The parade, which will also feature circus ring masters, will travel from Eyre Square to Spanish Arch, and is sure to delight audiences of all ages.

A Handful of Dreams

A Handful of Dreams is a mesmerizing outdoor performance that blends aerial and circus arts with live Irish traditional music and dance. The show, which features five aerial dancers and two live musicians, is a celebration of music, dance, and aerial acrobatics that will fill audiences with awe and warmth. No pre-booking is necessary for walk-up places.

Miracoco Luminarium

Architects of Air's luminarium is a walk-in sculpture that showcases light and colour in a breathtaking way. Designed by Alan Parkinson, these interactive walk-in sculptures have astounded audiences all over the world, from the Guggenheim in Spain to the Sydney Opera House in Australia. The luminarium features a dazzling maze of winding paths, soaring domes, and scintillating light, creating an enchanting and wondrous experience for people of all ages who enter it.
