Accommodations

Stay in the Heart of the City

Galway City Hostel

Overlooking Eyre Square and positioned right next to Galway's Rail and Bus Stations, the location is simply unbeatable. This Hostel also offers a free and substantial continental breakfast and a very comfortable shared lounge, as well as free Wi-Fi, printing, and luggage storage. Offering landmark and city views, each dorm bed is equipped with a privacy curtain, light, USB and power sockets, along with secure luggage storage.

Kinlay House Hostel

This top rated, award-winning Hostel is located in Eyre Square. Kinlay Hostel offers a mix of private rooms and dormitories. Guest facilities include a very comfortable common room with a pool table, 70" TV screen, a spacious and fully equipped self-catering kitchen and a large dining area. A free substantial breakfast, high-speed Wi-Fi & free luggage storage are just some of the benefits of staying here.

Sleepzone

Sleepzone is located in the centre of Galway and rated "Most Popular Hostel in Galway City" in 2019. Visitors have access to a large kitchen and dining area, a TV lounge, free Wi-Fi, and free safes. Enjoy complimentary coffee and tea all day!
