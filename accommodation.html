Accommodations

Stay in the Heart of the City

Galway City Hostel

This hostel, which provides free WiFi, breakfast, and luggage storage, is close to the rail and bus stations in Galway. Every convenience imaginable is offered.

Kinlay House Hostel

At Eyre Square, Kinlay Hostel has private rooms and dorms with free breakfast and Wi-Fi. Visitors can take advantage of a cosy common area with a TV and a fully functional self-catering kitchen.

Sleepzone

Galway's Sleepzone, which is in the city's centre, won the title of "Most Popular Hostel in Galway City" in 2019. A sizable kitchen and dining room, free Wi-Fi, and unlimited coffee and tea are available to visitors.
