Food

Delicious Meals on a Budget

Burgerstory

Burgerstory offers something for everyone, including delicious US-style burgers, chilli, wings and fries freshly prepared with the best of Irish ingredients.

McDonagh's

McDonagh's Fish and Chips Bar and Seafood Restaurant has been serving the best seafood for over 115 years. Enjoy mussels, scallops, salmon, or classic fish and chips either inside or outside!

Xi'an Street Food

Xi'an Street Food is an authentic Chinese restaurant located on Quay Street. Experience traditional hand-pulled noodles or try a Roujiamo, possibly the world's oldest sandwich!
